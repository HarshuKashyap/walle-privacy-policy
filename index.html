Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Introduction

WALLE is a health and wellness application designed to help users track symptoms, receive AI-powered insights, and manage personal health data securely and responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

  • Email address for account creation and authentication
  • Health-related inputs such as symptoms, preferences, and usage data
  • Anonymous analytics to improve app performance and reliability

3. Health Data Protection

We treat health-related information as sensitive personal data. All data is protected using industry-standard security measures including encryption and secure authentication.

4. Data Usage

User data is used strictly to provide app functionality, health insights, personalization, and service improvement. We do not sell personal or health data to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

WALLE uses trusted third-party services such as Firebase for authentication and secure data storage. These providers follow strict security and privacy standards.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share personal or health data with third parties without explicit user consent, except where required by law.

7. Account & Data Deletion

Users can delete their account at any time from the app settings. Once deleted, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems.

8. Children's Privacy

WALLE is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
