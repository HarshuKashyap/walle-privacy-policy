Privacy Policy – WALLE

Last updated: 2025

1. Introduction

WALLE is a health and wellness application designed to help users track symptoms, build healthier habits, and receive AI-powered wellness insights. We are committed to protecting user privacy and handling all data in a transparent and secure manner.

2. Information We Collect

  • Email address for account creation and authentication
  • Health-related inputs such as symptoms, wellness logs, and user preferences
  • Basic usage data to improve app stability and performance

3. Guest Usage

WALLE allows users to access limited features as a guest without creating an account. Guest users are not required to provide personally identifiable information. Some features may be restricted in guest mode.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information strictly to provide app functionality, personalize wellness insights, improve user experience, and maintain application security. We do not sell or misuse personal or health-related data.

5. Health Data Protection

Health-related information is treated as sensitive personal data. We apply industry-standard security practices, including encrypted communication and secure authentication, to protect user data from unauthorized access.

6. Medical Disclaimer

WALLE does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. All information and insights provided by the app are intended for general wellness and informational purposes only. Users should consult qualified healthcare professionals before making medical decisions.

7. Third-Party Services

WALLE uses trusted third-party services such as Firebase for authentication, secure data storage, and app performance monitoring. These services follow industry-standard privacy and security practices.

8. Data Sharing

We do not share personal or health data with third parties except when required by law or to protect user safety and application integrity.

9. Account & Data Deletion

Users can delete their account at any time through the app settings. Once an account is deleted, all associated personal and health data is permanently removed from our systems and cannot be recovered.

10. Children’s Privacy

WALLE is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
